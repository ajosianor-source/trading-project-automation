# Clinical data breach response

1. Open a privileged incident channel and preserve immutable evidence.
2. Contain identities, tokens, sessions, integrations, and exports; keep essential clinical services safe.
3. Determine data categories, patient population, jurisdictions, recipients, safeguards, and time window.
4. Privacy/legal owners perform HIPAA/GDPR notification analysis; engineering does not make this decision.
5. Eradicate the cause, rotate keys where exposure is plausible, and validate clean recovery.
6. Maintain a decision log, regulator/individual communications, chain of custody, and evidence access log.
7. Feed corrective actions into CAPA, DPIA, control matrix, training, and verification testing.
