# IoMT incident response

1. Preserve device certificate, attestation, firmware digest, network flow, and telemetry sequence evidence.
2. Quarantine the device VLAN and revoke its operational certificate without disrupting safety-critical care.
3. Engage clinical engineering and the clinical safety officer before isolation that may affect treatment.
4. Identify the model, firmware lot, affected sites, commands, patients, and safety hazards.
5. Block compromised firmware in the registry and rotate gateway credentials.
6. Assess reportability, including privacy breach, safety vigilance, and regulator/manufacturer notification.
7. Recover only to signed approved firmware, repeat attestation, and monitor for recurrence.
8. Link lessons learned to CAPA, threat model, risk file, SBOM, and post-market surveillance.
