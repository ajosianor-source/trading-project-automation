# Clinical, privacy, and operational governance

Joy Abu is the security administrator and accountable security owner. Production access is
time-bound, purpose-bound, MFA-protected, and reviewed quarterly. Break-glass access requires
an incident/change identifier and creates a high-severity immutable audit event.

PHI classification, de-identification, retention, and data-subject workflows require Privacy
Officer approval. Safe Harbor is a baseline only; rare disease, free text, imaging private
tags, small cohorts, and linked datasets require expert determination. Clinical safety,
FDA-regulated changes, and medical-device risk controls require independent approvers and
traceability from hazard through verification evidence.

Vendors require security review, a data-processing agreement/BAA where applicable, breach
notification terms, subprocessor visibility, data-location approval, exit testing, and annual
reassessment. Exceptions expire in at most 90 days and require owner, risk, compensating
controls, and executive acceptance.
